# Pricing Model

## Standard Offer

| Component | Price | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Setup | €1,000 one-time | Includes report + full build + deployment |
| Monthly (months 1–6) | €50/month | Introductory rate — must be stated in agreement upfront |
| Monthly (month 7+) | €100/month | Standard rate |
| Service fee | €75/hour | Structural changes, new features, new marketplaces |

**Total year 1:** €1,000 + (6 × €50) + (6 × €100) = **€1,900**
**Total year 2+:** €1,200/year

---

## What's Included

### Setup (€1,000)
- Opportunity report: how many products are in 1st place, how many are within reach and at what margin cost
- Full tool build and deployment
- Configuration of floor margin and ceiling margin per client
- Integration with their Worten Marketplace API key
- Initial run and validation that prices are updating correctly

### Monthly — Introductory (€50/month, months 1–6)
- Tool hosted and running on Pedro's server
- Monitoring — if it stops, Pedro fixes it
- Minor config changes (adjust floor/ceiling margins, pause specific products)
- Monthly check-in (optional) — "here's how many price adjustments ran this month"

### Monthly — Standard (€100/month, month 7 onwards)
- Everything above, at the standard rate
- Price increase disclosed and agreed upfront at signing — not a surprise

### Service Fee (€75/hour)
- Structural changes to repricing logic
- Adding a new marketplace (e.g. Phone House)
- New features or data sources
- Phone/video support beyond 30 min/month
- Emergency support outside business hours (minimum 1 hour billed)

---

## What's NOT Included in Monthly Fee

- Adding a second marketplace (e.g. Phone House) → €75/hour
- Structural changes to the logic (new repricing strategies, new data sources) → €75/hour
- Emergency support outside business hours → €75/hour, minimum 1 hour billed
- Phone/video support calls beyond 30 min/month → €75/hour

---

## Self-Hosting Option

If the client has a developer and wants to run it on their own infrastructure:

- Setup fee: €1,200 (€200 premium for full handover + documentation)
- No monthly fee after transfer
- No ongoing support included — support billed at €75/hour if needed

> Note: not recommended for clients without dedicated technical staff.

---

## Pilot Terms (first 3 clients)

- Same pricing as above
- In exchange: willingness to share results (anonymised) as a case study
- Direct access to Pedro for feedback during the first month
